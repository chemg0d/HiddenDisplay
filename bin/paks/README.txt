============================================
  HiddenDisplay - PAK Files
============================================

Place your VALORANT .pak mod files here.

Required files for each feature:

1. Blood & Corpse Display:
   - MatureData-WindowsClient.pak
   - MatureData-WindowsClient.sig

2. Japanese Voice Pack:
   - ja_JP_Text-WindowsClient.pak
   - ja_JP_Text-WindowsClient.sig

3. VNG Logo Removal:
   - VNGLogo-WindowsClient.pak
   - VNGLogo-WindowsClient.sig

NOTE: These .pak files must be sourced from your
VALORANT game installation. They cannot be generated.

your account. Use at your own discretion.
